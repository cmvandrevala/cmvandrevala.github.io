Thank You for Viewing Our Poster at the AAPT Conference!

I would like to use the following three videos to give you a taste of the CITA on CHIP system.

In the first video, we demonstrate a linear branching structure.

In the second video, we demonstrate the branching structure of the CITA system. Notice how the student has an option of two paths down which they can proceed. Additionally, notice how a student can return to any part of the previous analysis at any point during the problem. In fact, if the student has figured out how to complete the problem, he or she may exit the analysis early and enter their answer.

In the final video, the student takes the alternate branch of the problem. Notice how they now continue with analysis of angle rather than an analysis of length. No matter which path they take, they will get to the correct answer.

We just completed the beta test of our system during the summer 2015 semester. Starting this fall, we are going to analyze student data and make incremental improvements to CITA. We hope that over time, CITA will be an excellent tool for our students.
